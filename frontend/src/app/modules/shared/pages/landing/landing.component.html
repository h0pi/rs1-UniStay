What is UniStay?

UniStay is a comprehensive dormitory management system designed to streamline every aspect of student housing. From room selection and booking to maintenance requests and community announcements, we provide a seamless digital experience for both students and administrators.

Our mission is to create a comfortable, safe, and efficient living environment, allowing students to focus on their studies and enjoy their university life to the fullest.
